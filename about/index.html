About Us

Conveniently located across from the South Shore Harbour Resort & Conference Center, South Shore Eye Center has been serving the families of League City and the Bay Area for more than 30 years. The staff at South Shore Eye Center is made up of dedicated professionals committed to providing superior eye care and service with a personal touch. From performing routine eye exams and treating minor ocular emergencies to managing the complicated eyewear and eye care needs of astronauts, the team at South Shore Eye Center is experienced in all areas of optometric care.

In addition to practicing Therapeutic Optometry at South Shore Eye Center, Dr. Robert Gibson OD and Dr. Teresa Lutz OD maintain staff positions at:

  • Coastal Eye Associates – On staff at Coastal Eye Associates in Webster, Drs Gibson and Lutz are able to refer and co-manage complicated ophthalmological diseases and surgical cases with ophthalmologists who specialize in treating eye conditions including: cataracts, glaucoma, macular degeneration, pediatric disorders, eyelid procedures and diabetic retinopathy.
  • Advanced Laser Vision & Surgical Institute – As referring LASIK specialists, Drs Gibson and Lutz have been co-managing patients undergoing LASIK, PRK and intraocular lens implants, including ReSTOR, since ALV began serving patients in January, 2000. University of Houston, College of Optometry – For more than 15 years, Drs Gibson and Lutz have been Clinical Adjunct Professors at the UH College of Optometry and have mentored graduating optometry students in their practices.
  • University of Houston, College of Optometry – For more than 15 years, Drs Gibson and Lutz have been Clinical Adjunct Professors at the UH College of Optometry and have mentored graduating optometry students in their practices.
  • NASA’s Johnson Space Center – South Shore Eye Center has provided the eye care needs for astronauts of NASA, ESA (European Space Agency), JAXA (Japanese Aerospace Exploration Agency) and the (CSA) Canadian Space Agency for more than 7 years. We have served the complicated eyewear needs of astronauts flying on the International Space Station, the Space Shuttle and the Russian Soyuz. In addition, Dr. Gibson currently provides eye care in the Flight Medicine optometric clinic at Johnson Space Center and has published research, conducted at NASA, on the effects of space flight on the eye.

We hope you will consider us for all your eye care and eyewear needs. Please give us a call to schedule an appointment or stop by the office to view our extensive collection of designer eyewear.

MEET OUR DOCTORS

Dr. Robert Gibson

Dr. Robert Gibson received his Doctor of Optometry degree with honors from the University of Houston, College of Optometry and has maintained collaboration with NASA and worked as a Therapeutic Optometrist in the Bay Area for more than 30 years.

Dr. Robert Gibson grew up in the Panama Canal Zone and completed his undergraduate degree at the University of South Florida. He received his Doctor of Optometry degree with honors from the University of Houston, College of Optometry and has maintained collaboration with NASA and worked as a Therapeutic Optometrist in the Bay Area for more than 30 years.

Dr. Gibson currently provides eye care in the Flight Medicine optometric clinic at Johnson Space Center and has published numerous research articles, conducted at NASA, on the effects of space flight on the eye. In addition to practicing at NASA and South Shore Eye Center, Dr. Gibson is on staff at Coastal Eye Associates and Advanced Laser Vision & Surgical Institute, where he served as clinical director from 2000–2008. Dr. Gibson also serves as Clinical Adjunct Professor at the University of Houston, College of Optometry.

He and his wife, Lorraine, have one son. He enjoys golf, tennis and spending time with his family.

Watch this video by CNN: Dr. Gibson explains why astronauts could have eye problems returning from long missions in space.

Dr. Teresa Lutz

Dr. Lutz received her Doctor of Optometry degree with honors from the University of Houston, College of Optometry and has been working as a Therapeutic Optometrist in the Bay Area for more than 30 years.

Dr. Teresa Lutz grew up in Florida and graduated with honors to earn a BS in Nursing from the University of South Florida. Upon graduation, she moved to Houston to work in the organ transplant unit at Methodist Hospital and returned to school to study optometry at the University of Houston. She received her Doctor of Optometry degree with honors from the University of Houston, College of Optometry and has been working as a Therapeutic Optometrist in the Bay Area for more than 30 years.

In addition to practicing at South Shore Eye Center, Dr. Lutz is on staff at Coastal Eye Associates and Advanced Laser Vision&Surgical Institute. She serves as a Clinical Adjunct Professor at the University of Houston, College of Optometry and as a consultant to NASA Flight Medicine.
